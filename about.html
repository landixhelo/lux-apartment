Offering a garden and garden view, Lux Apartments is located in Ksamil, 400 metres from Sunset Beach and less than 1 km from Ksamil Beach. This beachfront property offers access to a balcony, free private parking and free WiFi. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk and a shared kitchen for guests.

Accommodation is fitted with air conditioning, a fully equipped kitchenette with a dining area, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with shower. At the apartment complex, all units are soundproof. At the apartment complex, all units are fitted with bed linen and towels.
